Vice

2018

 VICE explores how a bureaucratic Washington insider quietly became the most powerful
man in theworld as Vice-President to George W. Bush, reshaping the country and the globe
in ways still felt today.
Director:
Adam McKay
Cast:
Christian Bale as Dick Cheney
Amy Adam as Lynne Cheney
Sam Rockwell as George W. Bush
Steve Carell as Donald Rumsfeld
