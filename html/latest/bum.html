Bumblebee

2018

 On the run in the year 1987, Bumblebee finds refuge in a junkyard in a small Californian
beach town.Charlie, on the cusp of turning 18 and trying to find her place in the
world, discovers Bumblebee, battle-scarred and broken.

Travis Knight
